Design Brief

The goal is put together an interactive and informative design about the current pandemic sweeping the world. The design is light hearted while also being filled with helpful information covering Covid-19. The target audience is everyone and the anxieties we are dealing with during this troubling time. The graphics are not only to shed some light on the coronavirus but also what we can do to fill the time while in quarantine.

About this event

A Special Project During the pandemics : Dean Dale Merrill is hosting a contest for College of the Arts students. Feed your need for creativity in these unprecedented times by creating a short, innovative video showcasing dance, music, theatre, or visual arts and you could win $500 in cash!

requirements:

1:

Produce a video highlighting how you (or you and your fellow virtual collaborators) create dance, music, theatre, or visual arts content without meeting up (please maintain rules of social distancing during this time – stay healthy!). A link to your video must be submitted to one of the College of the Arts Social Media Accounts (Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter)

2:

Video content may not exceed 1 minute (60 seconds) in length.

3:

Videos must be submitted by 11:59 PM on Sunday, April 19, 2020. The winner will be announced on Friday, April 24. If you post your video on social media, be sure to tag the College of the Arts or use the #ArtTogetherNow hashtag! #csufcota @csufcota
