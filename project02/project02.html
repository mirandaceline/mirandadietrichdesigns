Design Brief

I. Purpose
The purpose of the designs is to promote and bring in an audience for the Activate 2020! event at the Begovich Gallery. The branding should let people know what the event is and get them excited for it.

II. Audience
The audience of the Activate2020! event are the CSUF students, staff, community, and anyone interested in the arts.

III. Message
The message is about creating excitment and action so that the audience will be engaged.

IV. Competition & Marketplace
Other local school art events and galleries like Glasgow School of Art at CSULB.

V. Context
The context will be displayed on digital and printed media to create excitement and movement between all the elements.

VI. Voice
This a one-night presentation of multiple installations and exhibitions situated in the Visual Arts courtyard and galleries.It is creative, playful, contemporary, and exciting

VII. Response
The audience should leave feeling creative and inspired.
