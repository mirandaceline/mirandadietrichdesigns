Four Favorite App

  1. Instagram

    Category: Social

    Rating: ★★★★☆

    I enjoy instagram because it is an app you can share who you are and what you enjoy with friends and family. If you are an artist you can even share your work and even make connections through it. You can also become inspired off others or even get a good laugh out of a funny meme you come across. It is a nice way to stay connected with world around us and what the people who inhabit it lives are like.

  2. Spotify

    Category:Music

    Rating: ★★★★★

    Spotify is my go to app for my music. I love it because you create a bunch of playlist for different genres of music and even create a playlist with someone where you can both add music to it. Also, when I’m sick of my saved music Spotify introduces you to new songs that you don’t have saved by creating radios based on your music taste. They also offer podcast stations with endless topics for whatever you feel like hearing about. Most of the time for me, it’s a murder mystery podcast.

  3. Etsy

    Category: Shopping

    Rating: ★★★☆☆

    Etsy is a beautiful app that lets small artist be creative and sell their work. I love it because you can’t find the products on Etsy anywhere else. They are so homemade and created with care by the artist. It also has great communication between the costumer and seller by eliminating the corruption aspects of it.

  4. Youtube

    Category: Video

    Rating: ★★★★☆

    Youtube is an endless array of varied videos for your entertainment. I can find myself on youtube for hours on end, clicking video after video. Most of the time I start from one video about makeup and end up watching video about the moon landing conspiracy. Youtube is just an endless hole for all sorts of video entertainment.
