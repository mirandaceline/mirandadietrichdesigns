About me

My name is Miranda Dietrich and I'm 24 years old. I was born in Orange, California and moved to Virgina Beach soon after. I was always an artistic kid dabbling in all forms of art. Art for me has always been a great way to express myself. It was until my high school art teacher would recommend I look into typography thus began my journey into the graphic design world. Other than art, my interest include living a meat free lifestyle and baking vegan goods. I also enjoy film photography as another hobbie.I express myself through my clothing. Working at a buy, sale, trade store, I come across a lot of unique items of clothing. That's where most of money goes toward. I have two amazing dogs named Winnie and Baby who I love very much. My life at the moment consist of school, work, and surrounding myself with amazing people.

My Design Philosophy

My design philosophy for Graphic Design would be to create meaningful designs that must use visual problem solving and critical thinking. I believe in hierarchy and a strong relationship between elements within my designs and how they interact with one another. I also believe that one must be extremely imaginative to create a design that will stand out among the rest but also be able to draw back on being overly ‘out there’. Overall, I think graphic design is all about balance to produce a design that is readable and interactive.
