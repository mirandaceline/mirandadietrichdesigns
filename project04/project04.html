Design Brief

 

(1) What is the purpose (goal) of the design?

The main purpose of these design is to create a web homepage with an intriguing design .

 

(2) Who is the audience?

The audience is mostly future clients.

 

(3) What message needs to be communicated?

The message that needs to be a showcase of who you are as a designer.

 

(4) What is the competition and marketplace?

The competition are other graphic designers in the same feild as you.

 

(5)What is the context?

The context is a navagation tool to get to know me as a designer.

 

(6) In what voice?

The voice is my own personal style out into the design layout.

 

(7) What kind of response is desired

To intrigue future clients into hiring me as a designer.
